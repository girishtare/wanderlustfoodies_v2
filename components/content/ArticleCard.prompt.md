Blog / travel-story card — full-bleed featured photo, floating category badge, editorial serif title, excerpt and a byline · date · read-time row. Lifts and zooms the photo on hover.

```jsx
<ArticleCard
  image="/assets/img/trek-sandakphu.jpg"
  category="trek" categoryLabel="Trek"
  title="Trekking with Friends: Off to Sandakphu!"
  excerpt="A spectacular Himalayan journey blending natural beauty, culture and panoramic mountains."
  author="Girish" date="Feb 9, 2025" readTime="26 min read"
  href="/post/sandakphu"
/>
```

Composes `Badge`. Place several in a responsive grid (min column ~320px).
