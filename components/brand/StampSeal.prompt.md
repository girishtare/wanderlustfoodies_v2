The brand's signature vintage rubber-stamp seal — arced top & bottom text around a center monogram, drawn purely with type and rings (no illustration). Use as a section divider, watermark, "approved" mark on recipes, or a hero accent.

```jsx
<StampSeal size={220} />
<StampSeal topText="HIMALAYA" bottomText="GARHWAL" center="2025" sub="THE HUMAN MIGRATION" />
<StampSeal center="WF" color="var(--paper-100)" background="var(--espresso-700)" />
```

Pass `color` / `background` to invert it onto dark sections.
